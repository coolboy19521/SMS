Who Are Whe?

Ahmed, Eyyub, Banu. As we witnessed the sadness of losing our closests in earthquake we decided to do something. We developped a project and called it SMS. Smart Monitoring System, which will help monitor all the information about earthquake. We hope our project will help people and no sadness will be witnessed.

What Is Our Project Doing?

How do we do it?

We installed gps and flex sensors. Flex sensors help us identify buildings that are broken during earthquake. We monitor broken buildings. Using GPS sensors we are able to know the coordinates of the broken buidling. After detecting which buildings are broken it is easier to find path. We also count number of people in a building. This way teams know how many people to rescue.
